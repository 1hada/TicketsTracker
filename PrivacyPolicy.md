# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2/14/2026  
**App Name:** Ticket-Tracker  
**Developer:** Sawgrass Snacks LLC  

---

## Overview

Ticket-Tracker respects your privacy. This app is designed to store and manage ticket and pass information locally on your device. We do **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

---

## Information We Collect

We do **not** collect personal information.

All data entered into the app — including names, email addresses, ticket details, usage history, and QR code data — is stored **locally on your device only**.

We do not operate servers and do not receive or store your data externally.

---

## How Data Is Stored

- All information is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage frameworks.
- Data remains under your control at all times.
- If you delete the app, your data may be permanently deleted unless included in a device backup.

---

## Import and Export Functionality

The app allows you to:

- **Export** ticket data as a JSON file.
- **Import** ticket data from a JSON file.

These files are created and managed entirely by you. We do not access, receive, or process exported data.

You are responsible for securely storing and sharing exported files.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:

- Share data with third parties  
- Sell personal data  
- Use advertising SDKs  
- Use analytics or tracking tools  
- Track users across apps or websites  

---

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services.

If you choose to share exported files using third-party apps (such as email or cloud storage providers), their privacy policies will apply.

---

## Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Your Rights and Control

Because all data is stored locally on your device:

- You may edit or delete your data at any time within the app.
- You may delete the app to remove stored data.
- You may export your data at any time for backup purposes.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Effective Date” above.
